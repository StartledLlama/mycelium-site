Who we are

A small studio with a simple idea

Mycelium is a six-person independent game studio. We started with one question: what if we just made games we actually want to play?

No chasing trends. No engagement loops designed to extract time. Just games with care put into every corner — because we'll be playing them too.

We hope to become a studio you trust. One game at a time.

Play first

Every decision starts with one question: "Is this fun?" If the team isn't enjoying it in playtests, it's not going in.

Honest craft

We'd rather ship a small, polished experience than a bloated one. Quality over scope, always.

No dark patterns

We don't build artificial friction or manipulative loops. Your time is yours — we just want to make it enjoyable.
