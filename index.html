Who we are

A small studio with a simple idea

Mycelium is a three-person independent game studio. We started with one question: what if we just made games we actually want to play?

No chasing trends. No engagement loops designed to extract time. Just games with care put into every corner — because we'll be playing them too.

Like mycelium itself, we grow quietly and connect things. Small team, honest work, games worth your time.

Play first

Every decision starts with the question: "Is this fun?" If the team isn't enjoying it in playtests, it's not going in.

Honest craft

We'd rather ship a small, polished experience than a bloated one. Quality over scope, always.

No dark patterns

We don't build artificial friction or manipulative loops. Your time is yours — we just want to make it enjoyable.
